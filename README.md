## About ISSA

The **Information Systems Security Association (ISSA)** is a global organization of information security professionals and practitioners dedicated to advancing individual growth, managing technology risk, and protecting critical information and infrastructure. ISSA provides members with education, networking, and professional development opportunities in the cybersecurity field.

## South Texas Chapter Overview

The **ISSA South Texas Chapter** serves the local cybersecurity community by:

- Promoting awareness and best practices in information security.
- Providing networking opportunities for security professionals, students, and industry leaders.
- Hosting local meetings, workshops, and conferences focused on cybersecurity trends and technologies.
- Encouraging professional development through certifications, training, and mentorship programs.
- Collaborating with local organizations, businesses, and educational institutions to strengthen regional cybersecurity posture.

## Activities and Programs

- **Monthly Meetings:** Discuss current cybersecurity trends, emerging threats, and case studies.
- **Workshops & Training:** Hands-on sessions covering tools, techniques, and frameworks.
- **Guest Speakers & Panels:** Insights from industry experts and security leaders.
- **Community Engagement:** Educational outreach programs and partnerships with local universities.
- **Mentorship & Networking:** Connect professionals and students for career growth.

## Membership

Membership is open to:

- Security professionals
- IT practitioners
- Students and educators
- Anyone passionate about cybersecurity

Benefits of joining:

- Access to local and global ISSA resources
- Professional networking opportunities
- Continuing education and certifications
- Participation in regional and global events
