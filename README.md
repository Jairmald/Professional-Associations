### About ISSA

The **Information Systems Security Association (ISSA)** is a global organization of information security professionals and practitioners dedicated to advancing individual growth, managing technology risk, and protecting critical information and infrastructure. ISSA provides members with education, networking, and professional development opportunities in the cybersecurity field.

### South Texas Chapter Overview

The **ISSA South Texas Chapter** serves the local cybersecurity community by:
- Promoting awareness and best practices in information security
- Providing networking opportunities for security professionals, students, and industry leaders
- Hosting local meetings, workshops, and conferences focused on cybersecurity trends and technologies
- Encouraging professional development through certifications, training, and mentorship programs
- Collaborating with local organizations, businesses, and educational institutions to strengthen regional cybersecurity posture

### Activities and Programs

- **Monthly Meetings:** Discuss current cybersecurity trends, emerging threats, and case studies
- **Workshops & Training:** Hands-on sessions covering tools, techniques, and frameworks
- **Guest Speakers & Panels:** Insights from industry experts and security leaders
- **Community Engagement:** Educational outreach programs and partnerships with local universities
- **Mentorship & Networking:** Connect professionals and students for career growth

### Membership

Membership is open to:
- Security professionals
- IT practitioners
- Students and educators
- Anyone passionate about cybersecurity

**Benefits of joining:**
- Access to local and global ISSA resources
- Professional networking opportunities
- Continuing education and certifications
- Participation in regional and global events

## ACFE (Association of Certified Fraud Examiners)

<img width="282" height="179" alt="ACFE Logo" src="https://via.placeholder.com/282x179?text=ACFE+Logo" />

### About ACFE

The **Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE)** is the world's largest anti-fraud organization and provider of the Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) credential. ACFE is dedicated to combating fraud and white-collar crime through education, certification, and professional development for fraud prevention and detection specialists.

### ACFE Mission

ACFE's core mission is to:
- Reduce the incidence and impact of fraud and white-collar crime
- Provide premier anti-fraud education and certification
- Support fraud prevention, detection, and investigation professionals
- Advance the field of fraud examination through research and best practices
- Foster a global community of fraud prevention specialists

### CFE Credential

The **Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE)** credential is recognized worldwide as the gold standard in fraud investigation and prevention.

**CFE Requirements:**
- Professional experience in fraud investigation, prevention, or detection
- Completion of coursework in fraud examination principles
- Passing comprehensive CFE examination
- Adherence to strict Code of Professional Ethics

**CFE Domains:**
1. Fraud Prevention and Deterrence
2. Fraud Investigation
3. Forensic Accounting
4. Legal Elements of Fraud

### ACFE Services & Resources

- **Training Programs:** Online and in-person courses on fraud prevention and investigation
- **Certification & Exams:** CFE examination preparation and administration
- **Publications:** Research reports, case studies, and anti-fraud best practices
- **Conferences & Events:** Annual fraud conference and local chapter meetings
- **Networking:** Connect with fraud investigation professionals worldwide
- **Continuing Professional Education (CPE):** Maintain CFE credentials through ongoing learning

### Membership Benefits

- Access to anti-fraud resources and research
- Discounted training and certification exam fees
- Professional networking and mentorship opportunities
- Industry publications and fraud trends reports
- Career advancement opportunities
- Participation in local and global ACFE chapters

### ACFE Chapters

ACFE operates hundreds of chapters globally, providing:

- Educational workshops and seminars

## Professional Development

Both ISSA and ACFE provide pathways for continuous professional development in cybersecurity and fraud examination:

- **Certifications:** CISSP, CISM, CEH (ISSA-aligned) and CFE (ACFE)
- **Education:** Structured training programs and coursework
- **Networking:** Connect with peers and industry leaders
- **Community:** Participate in local chapter activities
- **Resources:** Access to research, best practices, and industry insights
